What is Nyssa?

An extension to Blade package management

Nyssa is a simple abstraction on top of Blade's built-in package management facilities to provide a managed system of creating and consuming packages as well as manage their dependencies. Nyssa also provides you with a self-hostable package repository that can be used privately in organisations at large.
